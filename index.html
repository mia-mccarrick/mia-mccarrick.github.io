Application Procedures for Studying in China

STUDY IN CHINA

　　I  Entrance requirements

　　Welcome to choose China as the destination for your overseas study. Before submitting an application to a Chinese institution, please understand the admission requirements in advance:

　　If you apply for the admission to primary or secondary schools, please refer to  Regulations on Recruitment and Training of International Students ("School Recruitment and Training of International Students Management Measures") . 

　　If you apply to study in a college in China, please see the following requirements.

　　1. Basic requirements

　　*Non-Chinese citizens with a valid legal passport

　　*Good health

　　*No criminal record

　　*During your studies, you should abide by Chinese laws and regulations, respect Chinese customs and customs, and abide by school rules and regulations

　　*In principle, applicants must be at least 18 years of age; if an applicant is under 18 and his (her) parents do not dwell in China, the applicant"s parents shall entrust another foreign or Chinese citizens who live in China as the guardian of the applicant and provide relevant supporting materials.

　　*For those who have given up their original Chinese citizenship and become foreign citizens, the conditions set by the  Document No. 12 (2020) of Ministry of Education of the PRC Notice》) should be met.

　　3. Language requirements

　　*If you choose Chinese-taught course, you should have an appropriate Chinese proficiency Report(HSK scores for the Chinese proficiency test; if the applicant chooses the Chinese language training or preparatory courses, no HSK scores are needed).

　　*If you choose an English course, you should have the appropriate level of English (IELS or TOEFL scores, if the applicant"s native language is English, no IELS or TOEFL scores are needed)。
